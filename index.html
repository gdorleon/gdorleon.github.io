Dr. Gg Dorleon

Ginel is a Lecturer, AI Research Scientisit with a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from Paul Sabatier University, France. His work explores machine learning, algorithm bias, fairness, explainability, and responsible AI, with experience across academia and industry in France, Spain, the U.S., and Vietnam.
